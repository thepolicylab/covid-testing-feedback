The Policy Lab
Help improve the experience of COVID-19 testing

How should we locate, schedule, and encourage people to participate in testing for COVID-19?

The Policy Lab is working with state and local governments across the country to identify how the testing experience can better meet the needs of residents. Hearing directly from people who have been tested for COVID-19 (or who have attempted to get tested) is essential to this effort and we are grateful for your contribution.

To provide feedback, click a survey below that most closely matches where you are in the testing process. Each survey can take less than five minutes, though you’re welcome to provide more detailed information!

  • Deciding whether to get a test

  • Scheduled a test, but not yet gotten it

  • Currently waiting on results

  • Received results

If you’d like to provide feedback on the full testing experience, click here.

How we are using this information

The Policy Lab team will use aggregated survey responses to identify points where the testing process can be improved. We will share these findings with state decision-makers and other institutions who help implement COVID-19 testing. Your individual responses will remain anonymous, and no identifying information will be shared outside of the research team at The Policy Lab.
