Help improve the experience of Covid-19 testing

We want your feedback on locating, scheduling, and participating in testing for Covid-19

The Policy Lab at Brown University is working with state and local governments across the country to identify how the testing experience can better meet the needs of residents. Hearing directly from people who have been tested for COVID-19 (and who have attempted to get tested) is essential to this effort and we are grateful for your contribution.

To provide feedback, click a survey below that most closely matches where you are in the testing process. Each survey can take less than five minutes, though you’re welcome to provide more detailed information!

  • Deciding whether to get tested for Covid-19

  • Scheduling a test for Covid-19

  • Receiving a test for Covid-19

  • Receiving Covid-19 results and determining next steps

If you’d like to provide feedback on all four stages in one form, click here.

How we are using this information

The Policy Lab will use aggregated survey responses to identify points where the testing process can be improved. We will share these findings with state decision-makers and other institutions who help implement Covid-19 testing. Your individual responses will remain anonymous, and no identifying information will be shared outside of the research team at The Policy Lab.

For further questions, contact thepolicylab@brown.edu.
